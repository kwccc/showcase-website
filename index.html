About me

Hi, I’m Kai, an aspiring web developer passionate about learning, creating and collaboration. I started tech journey in a Fintech company that fostered a friendly, supportive environment where it was easy to ask questions and get help when stuck. That experience taught me the value of open knowledge-sharing and how much faster we grow when we learn together.

Why Web Development?

One of the things I love most about web development is how immediate and tangible it is. You write some code, refresh the page, and instantly see the result - it’s incredibly satisfying. There’s something special about turning an idea into something real that people can interact with.

Why Founders & Coders?

At Founders & Coders, I see a strong alignment with my own values: collaboration, knowledge-sharing, and community-driven learning. Having worked as both a mentor and a learner, I deeply appreciate the impact of peer learning and how projects thrive when multiple perspectives and skill sets come together. Some of the fastest and most effective learning experiences I’ve had have come from working with others; whether it’s learning from more experienced developers, mentoring those who are just starting out, or collaborating to break down complex concepts together.

What's next?

I want to continue growing as a developer while actively supporting those around me. My goal is to become a well-rounded web developer, contributing to exciting projects that make a real impact.

Collaboration is at the heart of great development, and I’m eager to be part of a community that thrives on learning together, supporting one another, and building something greater than the sum of its parts!
